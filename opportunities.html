Join the observatory

GigaFlor welcomes collaboration with students, researchers, institutions and conservation partners interested in Amazon giant forests.

MSc and PhD

Opportunities for graduate research on forest ecology, LiDAR, functional ecology, soil-vegetation interactions, climate vulnerability and spatial modeling.

Postdoctoral Research

Space for postdoctoral projects integrating field ecology, remote sensing, climate change, biodiversity and long-term ecological monitoring.

Field Expeditions

Training opportunities in forest inventory, environmental sensors, giant tree measurement, natural regeneration and conservation science.
