OPEN SCIENCE

Data, protocols and reproducible workflows

GigaFlor is prepared to host protocols, metadata, scripts, ecological datasets and spatial products related to Amazon giant forests.

Protocols

Field protocols for forest inventory, giant tree measurement, sensors, soil sampling, regeneration, dendrometric bands and LiDAR integration.

Metadata

Structured metadata for plots, trees, traits, environmental sensors, LiDAR transects, maps and laboratory analyses.

Spatial Products

Maps of canopy height, biomass, giant tree density, environmental gradients, climate vulnerability and monitoring sites.
