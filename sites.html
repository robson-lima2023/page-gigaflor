SITES

Monitoring Sites

Tumucumaque, Jari and the Amazonian landscapes where giant trees shape forest structure and resilience.

Tumucumaque National Park

One of the largest protected tropical forest areas in the world, providing highly conserved forests for long-term monitoring of giant trees, environmental gradients and biodiversity.

Jari Ecological Station

A strategic area for studying environmental gradients, hydrology, forest structure and giant tree dynamics in the Guiana Shield.

Interactive monitoring map coming soon

This area is reserved for the scientific map that will replace the template map. It can include plots, giant trees, LiDAR transects, sensors, soil points, hydrological gradients and conservation units.
